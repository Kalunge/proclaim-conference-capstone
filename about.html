Proclaim conference
2021

The Proclaim Conference this year on August 26th-28th. Our theme for the conference will be “Unreached: Reached Africa Reaching Unreached Africa”. The conference will be seeking to inform the attendants about the state of the great commission in Africa with the prayer that God will stir hearts and move churches to play their part in finishing the task. The Theme Verse for the conference will be Romans 10:14 (...how are they to believe in him of whom they have never heard?).
